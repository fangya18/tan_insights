About
About

Insight-driven consulting with scientific discipline.

Tan Insights was established to bridge the gap between advanced data science and the real-world complexity of clinical and epidemiological research. We work with pharmaceutical companies, academic medical centers, CROs, and public health agencies.

Our work spans the full data lifecycle — from study design and protocol development through statistical analysis, machine learning modelling, and final reporting.

We operate with the transparency and independence clients expect from a trusted scientific partner, not a vendor with competing interests.
